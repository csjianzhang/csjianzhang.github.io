Biography

Dr. Jian Zhang is an Assistant Professor (Ph. D. Supervisor) with the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering (SECE), Shenzhen Graduate School, Peking University, Shenzhen, China. He is now leading the Visual-Information Intelligent Learning LAB (VILLA) at PKU. His SECE webpage in Chinese is here .
